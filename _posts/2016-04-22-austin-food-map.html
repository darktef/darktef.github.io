I have lived in Austin for almost three years. There are tons of good food in Austin, my friend and I enjoyed exploring new and special restaurants during our spare time. Though I do not live in Austin anymore, I still miss the time with food there, and would love to share this feeling with other people visiting Austin.

Below is a map I curated for anyone, who is visiting Austin and wanna try some good restaurants there. I visited every restaurant listed below, and had a wonderful time during all my visits. Hope you guys will like it as well!

Icon made by Freepik from www.flaticon.com

  • Odd Duck

    Organic and fresh food, farm to table, small dishes, relative expensive, but really nice atmosphere/vibe.

  • Justine’s Brasserie

    Nice cocktail, kind relax and laid-back style

  • Dai Due

    Originally a butcher shop, then they start to cook...

  • Dolce Neve

    Popular during summer night when everybody just want gelato and late-night chat

  • Pete’s Dueling Piano Bar

    A famous piano bar to go at Austin

  • Musashino Sushi Dokoro

    They are expensive, but they have really good sashimi and sushi!

  • Uchi

    Everybody who loves Japanese food in Austin knows the name...

  • Caffe Medici

    My personal favorite coffee shop at Austin. Quiet neighborhood with great coffee!

  • Ramen Tatsu-Ya

    Best Ramen in the town, people wait in lines for it. Recommend sweet and sour Yoda for sides~
